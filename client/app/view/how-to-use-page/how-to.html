How to use GitConnect

Home

See your network activity and how many developers are available nearby.

Connect

Match with other developers.

Our algorithm gets your interests based on your GitHub activity and proposes developers with similar interests.

Projects

See published projects made by other GitConnect users.

You can rate, comment, and get in touch with their team.

Connections

See and manage your connections with other members.

My Projects

See your published projects, and projects currently in progress.

Add a Project

Simply click the plus icon to create a new project.

Add developers you are connected with and collaborate.

Requests

See and manage the connection requests you've sent or received from other members.

Messages

Keep in touch with other members by sending and receiving messages.

Check out the latest projects!
